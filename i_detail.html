How to Become an Interior Designer in India

Career in Interior Designing

Do you always work in making the interior of your room look appealing? Are you always spellbound by the interiors of hotels, buildings, apartments, etc.? If yes, you are already on the track of becoming an interior designer.

An interior designer works with clients to create visually pleasing spaces...

What are the Roles and Responsibilities of an Interior Designer?

  • Interior designers make indoor areas functional, safe, and attractive by efficient use of spaces and decorative items.
  • Advise clients on interior design factors such as space planning, layout, and furnishings.
  • Use computer-aided drafting (CAD) and related software to produce construction documents.
