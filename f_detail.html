How to Become a Fashion Designer in India

Career in Fashion Designing

Are you fascinated by the latest fashion trends? Do you want to design designer clothes? Does the feeling of your designs being shown in a fashion week give you the rush? If yes, you should consider a career in fashion design.

A fashion designer designs creative clothes, accessories, and jewelry. Passion is the key to success in the field of fashion designing...

What are the roles and responsibilities for Fashion Designers?

  • Fashion designers design garments that are functional and attractive.
  • They make sure to keep with trends, market predictions, and climate.
  • They know fabric, draping techniques, material, and color trends.
  • They sketch original ideas on paper and modify garments to achieve desired effects.
